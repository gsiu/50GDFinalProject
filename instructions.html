How To Play

On an Android 2.x device, when the Sky High app is launched, a start page appears.
Touch the balloon with the red X to exit the game, touch the trophy to view a list of high scores, or touch the green play button to start the game.

Objective/Goal:

The goal of the game is to fly the balloon has high as possible into the sky without popping it.
The balloon is popped if it collides with any enemy objects flying in the sky. If the balloon pops, the game is over.
Your score reflects the number of seconds the balloon has been flying in the sky.

Game Controls:

Make sure the accelerometer on the device is activated.
Move the balloon from left to right by tilting the device to one side or the other.

Enemies:

Move the balloon to the left and right in order to avoid enemies.
Enemies include airplanes, missiles, and birds.
Be aware that the higher your score, the more frequently enemies appear.

Power-ups:

If the balloon collides with a power-up, the balloon will be invincible for 5 seconds (if it collides with an enemy, it will not pop).
